What it does

SongLens combines song study, guitar learning, and POD Go tone guidance so users can understand the music and get closer to the sound.

Learn

Identifies the song’s likely themes, meaning, emotional tone, and message from a shared link or song title.

Play

Suggests guitar learning focus such as clean rhythm, ambient lead, worship swells, gain staging, and timing feel.

Configure

Provides practical Line 6 POD Go starting-point settings for amp, cab, EQ, effects, delay, and reverb.
